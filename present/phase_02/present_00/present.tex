                \item \textbf{Seed Incubation Plant} aims to optimize the germination and
                    growth process of seeds by providing a controlled and
                    monitored environment.

                \item \textbf{Environment Monitoring and Control Unit} (EMCU) part of the
                    incubator takes care of environmental factors such as temperature,
                    humidity, lighting, $\mbox{O}_2$, etc.

                \item \textbf{Growth Monitoring Unit} (GMU) closely monitors and collects growth related
                    information from each of the germinating saplings.

                \item With the help of \textbf{SINC}, a companion app, the status of the incubator
                    can be closely watched.
            \end{itemize}
